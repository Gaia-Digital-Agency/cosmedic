# Cosmedic CMS — User Guide
**For:** the clinic content team · **Updated:** 2026-05-29
**Login URL:** https://cosmedic.gaiada.online/admin

> The CMS is where you edit everything on the website — text, images, prices, doctors, blog. Changes go live within ~1 minute. You never touch code.

---

## 🔑 Log In
1. Go to **https://cosmedic.gaiada.online/admin**
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.
3. Click **Login**. You land on the Dashboard (cards grouped by section).
- Forgot password? Use **"Forgot your password?"** on the login screen — a reset link is emailed to you.

## 🚪 Log Out
- The **exit icon (↩ door)** sits at the **very bottom of the left sidebar**, below all the sections.
- Click it to sign out. (You're auto-signed-out after a period of inactivity.)

## 👤 Avatar — what clicking it does
Click your **avatar / initials** (top of the sidebar) to open your **Account** page. There you can:
| In Account | What it does |
|------------|--------------|
| **My Account** | Change your own email and password |
| **Theme** | Switch **Light / Dark / Auto** — the CMS now defaults to **Light** |
| **Language** | Switch the admin interface language |
| **Log out** | Sign out (same as the sidebar exit icon) |

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 User (Collections → Users)
The **Users** list is the team who can log in. Open a user to edit:
| Field | What it is |
|-------|-----------|
| **email** | Login email address |
| **password** | Login password (set/reset here) |
| **role** | Permission level (e.g. admin / editor) |
| **avatar** | Profile picture shown in the sidebar |
> Note: the **Theme toggle is NOT here** — it's on your **Account** page (click your avatar).

## 💡 Quick tips
- **Images:** click an image field → upload or pick from the Media Library. Add **alt text** (describe the image) for accessibility.
- **Prices:** only enter the **IDR** price — the AUD figure is calculated automatically from the exchange rate in **Settings**.
- **Save:** every edit needs **Save**. The live site updates within ~1 minute.
- **Dark card = site-wide setting** (a Global); **light card = a list of records** (a Collection).
- **Can't find a field?** Most rarely-changed/technical fields are hidden on purpose — ask a developer to unhide if you truly need it.
